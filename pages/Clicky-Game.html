Don't Click On The Same Image Twice

Users click on images that change order after each click. The objective is to click on all of the images without clicking on the same image twice. For each pick that hasn't been guessed, the user earns one point. If the user clicks on an image that they have already picked, their score resets to 0. A maximum of 12 points can be earned. I themed this game after one of my favorite musicians, Miles Davis.
